What is CSE 152?

CSE 152 is a class about computer vision, i.e. acquiring meaning from images, i.e. teaching computers to see, to perform the visual processing that humans are capable of and more.

We will cover the topics shown in the course roadmap to the right. Excitingly, Prof. Su is updating the course this semester to provide a more comprehensive (including modern) CV foundation, with topics such as normalized cuts, semantic segmentation, and deep learning.
